01 · cloud.nine A BOS. product line · 2025

cloud.nine

New Seasonal Product Line Development · BOS., 2025

Product Owner · Brand Experience Lead · Campaign Coordination

After nearly a decade on the market, BOS. had built strong credibility among specialty coffee enthusiasts. However, the brand remained less approachable to younger audiences seeking softer, more lifestyle-oriented F&B experiences.

To expand the brand experience while maintaining the "Truly Specialty" spirit, the team developed cloud.nine, a seasonal product line designed to introduce a more emotional, approachable, and visually expressive side of BOS.

The first season spoke in three flavors, each shaped into a feeling:

  • Smoked Oak (Gỗ Sồi) for confidence - tự tin
  • Salted Caramel (Caramen Muối) for love - yêu & được yêu
  • Elder Flower (Hoa gạo) for determination - quyết tâm

Three feelings a new audience could find a part of themselves in.
